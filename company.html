Ashabhai & Company Pte Limited (the Company) was incorporated in August 1977 as a limited liability company in Fiji Islands, with its Registered Office and Head Office located at Lot 1, Karsanji Street, Bhindi Industrial Sub-division, Vatuwaqa, GPO Box 908, Suva. Initially, the organization operated as a partnership business from 1957 to 1967 and later as a sole trading business, owned and managed by Mr. Ashabhai Parshotam Patel, until 1977. The Company has branches strategically located in Lautoka, Ba, and Labasa, serving different regions effectively. The Company employs over 480 people with a cohesive organizational structure. Its principal business activities include import, export, wholesale, and distribution of fast-moving consumer goods, dealing in over 1000 SKUs of quality products.

Additionally, the Company exports to Pacific countries and distributes products Fiji-wide through its own fleet of vehicles. It sources products globally, enabling it to serve various establishments such as supermarket chains, resorts, restaurants, hospitals, and offices. Subsidiary companies under Ashabhai & Company Pte Limited include Novel Distributors Pte Limited, Ashabhai Talkzone Pte Limited, Western Dairy Pte Limited, and Utopia International Trading Pte Limited. Novel Distributors Pte Limited, a fully owned subsidiary, engages in manufacturing activities, producing baking products, snacks, and repackaging consumer products for domestic and international markets. Western Dairy Pte Limited, based in Nadi, manufactures yogurt and juice products.

Throughout its over six-decade journey, the Company has grown and evolved, focusing on customer satisfaction, prudent funds management, and continuous process improvement. It has received recognition such as the Achievement in Business Excellence Award 2010 for its high service standards and ethical operations. The Company is managed by an experienced team of employees led by a proficient Board of Directors, including Prakash Patel, Harish Patel, Jayant Patel, and Lalitaben A. Patel. Each director brings extensive knowledge and expertise to the Company, ensuring its success and credibility in the market.
